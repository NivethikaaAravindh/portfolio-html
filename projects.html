TITLE: MECHANIZED VALVE CONTROLLER USING ARDUINO

In this system, the sensors (Infra-red) will sense the entry and exit of an object and automatically opens the outlet valve. This project mainly concentrates on water management system. This helps to reduce the wastage of water in public managements.

TITLE: SMART WATER METER USING IoT

This system helps in finding the water theft happening in the locals, in this the water is been monitored using flow meter and microcontroller ,that datas are stored in iot,when there is a change in flow the solenoid valve will automatically get closed.
